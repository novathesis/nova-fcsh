# Abstract: nova-fcsh-novathesis-v7.10.1

This is a customized LaTeX template for theses and reports at **Faculdade de Ciências Sociais e Humanas da Universidade NOVA de Lisboa (NOVA FCSH)**, following the most recent [NOVA FCSH guidelines (in Portuguese, requires atuthentication)](https://intranet.fcsh.unl.pt/formularios-e-modelos-academicos). 

This template is a streamlined and fine-tuned version of the robust **NOVAthesis framework (v7.10.1)**, trimmed down and optimized to provide NOVA FCSH students with a streamlined experience, meeting NOVA FCSH's academic formatting standards without the complexity of the original multi-institution template. 

### Key Features:

* **NOVA FCSH-Ready:** Pre-configured with the correct margins, cover page styles, and fonts required by NOVA FCSH.
* **Streamlined:** Unnecessary files and configurations for other universities and languages (other than PT and EN) have been removed, making the template lighter and easier to navigate.
* **Modern Academic Standards:**
    * **AI Disclosure:** Integrated support for the new Artificial Intelligence Disclosure Statement.
    * **SDGs:** Visual enumeration of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) for modern research impact reporting.
* **User-Friendly Design:** Optimized for both LaTeX beginners and power users, ensuring a clean and efficient workflow.
